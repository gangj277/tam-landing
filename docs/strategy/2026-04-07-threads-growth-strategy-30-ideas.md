# TAM Threads Growth Strategy - 30 Post Ideas

Date: 2026-04-07  
Goal: grow a high-trust Threads account around `AI era parenting + self-understanding + education change`, not overt app promotion  
Primary audience: parents of children roughly ages `10-14`  
Secondary audience: teachers, counselors, education-curious operators

## 1. Strategic Thesis

TAM should not sound like `an app account trying to get users`.
It should sound like `the sharpest account on Korean Threads for understanding what children need in the AI era`.

That means:

- `70%` parent pain + education reframing
- `20%` data-backed contrarian takes
- `10%` builder/founder worldview with subtle TAM energy

The product should appear as a consequence of the worldview, not the opening claim.

## 2. Threads-Native Rules

Trend check as of `2026-04-07`:

- Meta said on `October 21, 2024` that replies account for almost half of views on Threads, so every post should invite an easy first reply.
- Meta said on `October 21, 2024` that posting `2-5` times per week is associated with higher impressions per post.
- Meta said on `October 21, 2024` that posts with text on photo/video/carousel tend to get more views than media without text.
- Meta said on `April 24, 2025` that posts with tagged topics generally get more views than posts without one.
- Meta launched text attachments on `September 4, 2025`, which makes longer educational mini-essays native to Threads instead of screenshot essays.

Operating rules:

- Lead with tension, not explanation.
- End with a question that can be answered in one sentence.
- Keep most posts as `single post` or `short thread`; use `text attachment` only when nuance is the point.
- Use one topic tag when it is truly relevant: `교육`, `학부모`, `고교학점제`, `AI교육`, `진로교육`, `중학생`, `디지털습관`.
- Add a first follow-up reply within `10 minutes` with either:
  - one concrete example
  - one counterexample
  - one mini framework
- Do not lead with TAM screenshots or product demo posts while the account is still small.

## 3. Media Playbook

Only attach media when it helps the post skim faster.

Use these image types:

- `Stat card`: one number + one sentence
- `Myth vs reality`: two-panel comparison
- `Mini framework`: 3-step checklist or 2x2
- `Routine graphic`: simple day timeline or weekly pattern card
- `Low-polish note`: text attachment or note-style image for intimate founder takes

Avoid:

- polished promo graphics
- generic stock family photos
- product UI as the hero image
- carousels with too much text per slide

## 4. Content Mix

Recommended weekly mix:

- `2` sharp single posts
- `1` short thread
- `1` poll or reply-bait post
- `1` longer text attachment every `1-2` weeks

Suggested cadence:

- Tuesday: contrarian education take
- Thursday: parent pain / practical reframe
- Saturday or Sunday: poll, story, or founder raw take

## 7. Soft Promotion Rules

Do not do this in the opening post:

- `저희 앱은...`
- `탐에서...`
- `링크는 바이오에...`
- direct feature demo

Do this instead:

- post the insight first
- answer replies with one concrete framework
- only mention the product when someone asks `그럼 이런 건 어떻게 도와주나요?`
- once every `8-10` posts, allow one subtle builder post or one anonymized example

## 8. Reusable CTA Bank

Use questions that are easy to answer fast:

- `이 말에 동의하시나요, 아니면 너무 과한 주장 같나요?`
- `아이에게서 최근 반복해서 본 패턴 하나가 있나요?`
- `이 주제에서 제일 막막한 부분은 어디인가요?`
- `부모 입장에서 가장 먼저 바꾸고 싶은 질문이 있나요?`
- `학교보다 집에서 더 어려운 건 뭐였나요?`

Avoid:

- `어떻게 생각하세요?`
- `공감하시면 좋아요`
- `여러분의 의견을 자유롭게 남겨주세요`
